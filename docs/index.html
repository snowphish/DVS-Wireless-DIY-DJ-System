DVS Kit Flasher

Flash your board right from this page — no software to install. Works in Chrome or Edge on desktop.

This browser can't flash over USB. Open this page in desktop Chrome or Edge (Web Serial isn't in Safari or Firefox).
  1. Plug the board into this computer with a data USB-C cable (charge-only cables won't work).
  2. On a battery-powered puck, switch it OFF first — flash over USB only.
  3. Click Connect & Flash, pick the board's serial port, and wait for "flashing complete."
  4. To pair: power the receiver first (its LED blinks blue for about a minute), then switch on each puck — the first becomes deck 1, the second deck 2. No other setup needed; hold the receiver's re-pair button (~2 s) to redo pairing anytime.

Compatible with Serato and Traktor timecode. Not affiliated with or endorsed by Serato or Native Instruments.
